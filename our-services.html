The Pride of
Real Estate

OA Real Estate Group was born from the success of our sister company Open Air Homes, a high-end short-term rental company. The business has flourished as a result of impeccable customer service, focus on design, comfort and outdoor space which makes California so special and keeps guests returning for more. What makes our group different from other real estate groups is our ability to partner with Open Air Homes to help monetize your property with short-term rental bookings while we sell your property at the same time.

Our mission is to provide nothing less than phenomenal representation and individualized attention to both homebuyers and sellers. Our commitment to consistent communication, timeliness and resourcefulness is our top priority to our clients and we will work tirelessly seven days a week to achieve the desired results. The OA Real Estate Group, backed by the resources of luxury real estate firm Douglas Elliman, has the ability to expose each property to a global network with discretion, class and innovative marketing that will help get your property sold. We specialize in homes of all styles and price points and our knowledge of the market throughout Los Angeles' diverse neighborhoods allows us to help serve our clients best. For sellers, our top goal is to price your home competitively and sell it at the highest price possible in the shortest amount of time.

For buyers, our goal is to narrow down, flag and show properties that fit your criteria until one is found and help you navigate the purchase process which sometimes involves multiple offers, a norm in our current market. We look forward to building a fruitful relationship that exceeds all of your expectations. Please contact us for a consultation today and let's explore your real estate options and goals.
