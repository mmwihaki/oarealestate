About
Austin

Austin began his career in real estate after working under one of the top producing agents in Los Angeles. This experience allowed him to gain the knowledge necessary to serve his clients at the highest level.

Being born and raised in California with a deep love for Los Angeles, he found a career in real estate to be the perfect fit. Austin seamlessly transitioned to real estate after previously working as a national and international male model and actor based in Los Angeles. The skills he learned in that industry helped him succeed at working with new clients, quickly adjusting based on their wants and needs to get a successful end goal.

Austin prides himself on putting his clients first and doing whatever it takes to have a smooth and rewarding transaction. No pressure, no sales, just honest and clear communication. His goal is to handle the entire transaction and to let his clients sit back and relax.

Austin is a part of the Beverly Hills Greater Los Angeles Association of Realtors, the California Association of Realtors and the National Association of Realtors.
