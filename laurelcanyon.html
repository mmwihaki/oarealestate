Property Description

This home is bursting with character throughout the entire property. This updated classic maintains all of its iconic charm while embracing modern design updates that make staying here an absolute breeze. Ideal for larger groups, enjoy maid service and vast outdoor space for hosting gatherings by the pool and living out your Hollywood moment in style.


Nestled off of Laurel Canyon, this beautiful home offers everything you could need to relax in Los Angeles, all while being centrally located to be able to enjoy everything Los Angeles has to offer. Some of the highlights include a large heated pool, hot tub, sauna, waterfall, outdoor entertaining space, Sonos music system, gas BBQ, and on-property parking for 3 cars.


One of the biggest perks of this home is that it is a 30-second walk away from the legendary Canyon Country Store. You can grab almost anything during your stay last minute, from an amazing latte to sandwiches or alcohol. The Canyon Country Store is a local favorite, and having it a 30-second walk away allows you the opportunity to enjoy this beautiful house without ever having to jump in your car and deal with the Los Angeles traffic!
