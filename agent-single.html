Hunter Burgarella

Hunter started his real estate career full steam ahead as he oversaw 24 sales and 40 leases totalling $40M during his time as Director of Operations for a top real estate broker in Los Angeles. The role allowed him to gain vast amounts of knowledge in all aspects of real estate from commercial, to probate, trust and income transactions. He quickly grasped a strong knowledge of the market and excelled at client relations. Hunter prides himself most on his strong relationships with his clients. His integrity, accessibility, honest assessment, attention to detail, organization and overall personal touch make clients feel valued and attended to.
